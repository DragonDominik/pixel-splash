# Pixel Splash

A simple and fun pixel art web app built with **Tailwind CSS** and **JavaScript**. Draw pixel art directly in your browser, everything is saved automatically to **localStorage** while you create.

**Live Demo:** [https://dragondominik.github.io/pixel-splash/](https://dragondominik.github.io/pixel-splash/)

---

## Features

- **Click & Drag Drawing**: Draw in cells easily by clicking or dragging your mouse.  
- **Recent Colors**: Your last 10 colors are saved for quick access.  
- **Color Picker**: Grab colors directly from your canvas.  
- **Customizable Canvas**: Adjust canvas size and border width. On resizing, an extra 10 rows and columns are saved to preserve existing drawing data.  
- **Copy to Clipboard**: Quickly copy your drawing.

On resizing the canvas saves +10 cols and rows data

> **Note:** Maximum supported canvas size is **500x500**, though drawing experience is not fully supported at that size.
